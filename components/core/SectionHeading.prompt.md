**SectionHeading** — eyebrow label, serif title, divider, and optional lead paragraph. Opens most page sections.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  eyebrow="Nuestros servicios"
  title="Manos y pies de revista"
  lead="Manicura, pedicura y estética con producto premium e higiene certificada."
/>
```

`align="left"` for asymmetric layouts, `tone="light"` on dark backgrounds, `divider={false}` to hide the motif.
